About me

Finance meets
Data & Story

I'm a Data Analyst at Morningstar, where I work on the Lumonic team extracting and standardizing global financial statement data — Balance Sheets, Income Statements, and Cash Flow — to support investment research products used by analysts and institutions worldwide.

I hold an M.A. in Business Economics (CGPA 9.90) from Mumbai University and a B.A. in Economics (CGPA 8.96). Previously I was a Financial Analyst at Atos Syntel, where I earned the Champ Award in 2023.

Beyond my day job, I run two blogs, a YouTube channel, and build educational content for SSC CGL aspirants — all aimed at making finance and data accessible to everyone.
