// Study Plan

Recommended Strategy

How to approach your CGL prep for maximum marks in minimum time.

Week 1–4

Build the Base

Start with Maths (Number System → %) and GS (History → Polity). These take the longest to internalize. Do 1 chapter per day.

Week 5–7

Reasoning + English

Reasoning is quick wins — spend 2 days per topic, solve 20 questions each. English: grammar rules first, vocab daily (10 words/day).

Week 8

Static GK + Computer

These are memory-based. Read tables daily, revise the next morning. Computer shortcuts can be learned in 3 days.

Week 9–12

Revision + Mocks

Revise all formula sheets and notes. Attempt full-length mocks every alternate day. Analyse every mistake.

Ongoing

Daily Targets

25 Maths Qs + 15 Reasoning Qs + 10 English Qs + 10 GS facts every single day. Consistency beats intensity.

Exam Day

Order of Attempt

Reasoning (fastest) → English → GS → Maths (last, time-consuming). Never leave any section untouched.
